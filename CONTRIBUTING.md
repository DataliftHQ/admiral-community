This repository is the central hub for feedback, discussions, and community support for Admiral. While Admiral itself is closed-source SaaS, we deeply value community input and collaboration.

## Ways to Contribute

### üêõ Report Bugs

Found something that's not working? Help us fix it!

1. **Search first:** Check [existing issues](https://github.com/DataliftHQ/admiral-community/issues) to avoid duplicates
2. **Use the template:** [Create a bug report](https://github.com/DataliftHQ/admiral-community/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml) with clear reproduction steps
3. **Be specific:** Include version numbers, environment details, and error messages
4. **Add context:** Screenshots, logs, and configuration snippets help immensely

**What makes a great bug report:**

- Clear, descriptive title
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Expected vs. actual behavior
- Environment details (Admiral version, Kubernetes version, cloud provider)
- Relevant logs or error messages

### üí° Suggest Features

Have an idea to make Admiral better?

1. **Check existing ideas:** Browse [Ideas discussions](https://github.com/DataliftHQ/admiral-community/discussions/categories/ideas)
2. **Start a discussion:** Share your idea in [Ideas](https://github.com/DataliftHQ/admiral-community/discussions/new?category=ideas) first to gather feedback
3. **Explain the problem:** What problem does this solve? Who benefits?
4. **Describe the solution:** How would you like Admiral to work?
5. **Consider alternatives:** What other solutions have you considered?

**From idea to feature:**

- Ideas discussion ‚Üí Community feedback ‚Üí Roadmap consideration ‚Üí Development
- Upvote features you want to see (we prioritize based on community interest)
- We'll move popular ideas to tracked issues when we commit to building them

### üôã Answer Questions

One of the best ways to contribute!

- Browse [Q&A discussions](https://github.com/DataliftHQ/admiral-community/discussions/categories/q-a)
- Share your knowledge and experience
- Help fellow Admiral users solve problems
- We appreciate detailed answers with examples

### üéâ Share Your Story

Help others learn from your experience:

- Post in [Show and Tell](https://github.com/DataliftHQ/admiral-community/discussions/categories/show-and-tell)
- Share your Admiral setup, architecture, or use case
- Write blog posts, tutorials, or guides
- Present at meetups or conferences

### üìñ Improve Documentation

Spot a typo or confusing explanation in our docs?

- Documentation lives at [admiral.io/docs](https://admiral.io/docs)
- Report issues via [bug report](https://github.com/DataliftHQ/admiral-community/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml) and select "Documentation"
- Suggest improvements in [Discussions](https://github.com/DataliftHQ/admiral-community/discussions)

## Community Guidelines

### Be Respectful

- Treat everyone with respect and kindness
- Welcome newcomers and help them learn
- Assume good intent in discussions
- Disagree constructively and professionally

### Stay On Topic

- Keep discussions focused on Admiral
- Use appropriate discussion categories
- Don't hijack threads with unrelated topics
- Take sidebar conversations to new threads

### Be Clear and Concise

- Use descriptive titles for issues and discussions
- Provide context and details
- Format code blocks properly with syntax highlighting
- Include relevant links and references

### No Spam or Self-Promotion

- Don't post unsolicited advertisements
- Share relevant tools/services only when genuinely helpful
- Disclose affiliations when recommending products
- Respect that this is an Admiral community space

## What We're Looking For

### High-Value Contributions

We especially appreciate:

- **Detailed bug reports** with clear reproduction steps
- **Well-researched feature requests** that explain the problem and use case
- **Helpful answers** to community questions
- **Real-world examples** and use cases
- **Constructive feedback** on new features
- **Tutorials and guides** that help others succeed

### Recognition

We recognize valuable contributors by:

- Highlighting helpful answers and great discussions
- Featuring community content in our newsletter
- Crediting contributors in release notes (when appropriate)
- Building relationships with active community members

## Response Times

**What to expect:**

- **Critical bugs:** 24-48 hours
- **General bugs:** 2-5 business days
- **Feature requests:** Acknowledged within a week, evaluated monthly
- **Questions:** Community often responds within hours; team within 2-3 days
- **Discussions:** Ongoing, as schedules permit

**Enterprise customers** should use your dedicated support channels for urgent issues.
